=head1 About

This is my personal collection of L<Project Euler|http://projecteuler.net/>
solutions. I started working on these problems as a way to learn Perl. They're
not the most efficient or beautiful solutions, but they did teach me a thing or
two about L<map|http://perldoc.perl.org/functions/map.html>,
L<grep|http://perldoc.perl.org/functions/grep.html>,
L<List::Util|http://perldoc.perl.org/List/Util.html>,
and the
L<Sieve of Eratosthenes|https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sieve_of_Eratosthenes>.
